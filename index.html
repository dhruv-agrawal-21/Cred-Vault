One Platform. Complete Asset Control.

Cred Vault manages the entire fixed asset lifecycle — from purchase and capitalization to transfer, depreciation, and disposal. Replace fragmented records with a single source of truth.

  • Maintain a centralized Fixed Asset Register (FAR)
  • Track assets by location, department, and cost centre
  • Automate depreciation calculations
  • Monitor asset movement and utilization
  • Stay compliant and audit-ready
